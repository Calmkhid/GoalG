Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are your predictions?

Our free predictions have a success rate of approximately 70%, while our VIP predictions achieve around 85% accuracy. Our VVIP elite predictions can exceed 90% success rates based on historical data.

How do I upgrade to VIP membership?

You can upgrade to VIP by visiting our VIP page and selecting a subscription plan that works for you. You'll need to create an account if you don't already have one.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

VVIP memberships come with a 7-day money-back guarantee. For VIP memberships, refunds are considered on a case-by-case basis. Please contact our support team to discuss your situation.

How do I receive the predictions?

Free predictions are available directly on our website. VIP and VVIP predictions are accessible in your member account dashboard and are also sent via email. VVIP members also receive SMS notifications for high-confidence tips.
