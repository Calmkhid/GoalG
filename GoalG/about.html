Our Story

Goal Genius was founded in 2020 by a team of football analysts, data scientists, and passionate fans with a mission to revolutionize football predictions.

We noticed that many prediction sites were making random guesses without any solid analytical foundation. This led us to create a data-driven platform that utilizes advanced algorithms, statistical models, and expert analysis to provide the most accurate football predictions possible.

Today, Goal Genius has grown into a trusted community of over 100,000 users who rely on our predictions to make informed betting decisions.

Our Approach

Historical Data Analysis

We analyze years of historical match data, including team performance, head-to-head records, player statistics, and more to identify patterns and trends.

Advanced Algorithms

Our proprietary algorithms combine machine learning and statistical models to generate predictions with high accuracy rates.

Expert Analysis

Our team of football experts reviews algorithmic predictions, adding contextual factors like injuries, team news, and tactical insights.

Continuous Improvement

We continuously refine our models based on actual results, ensuring our prediction accuracy improves over time.
